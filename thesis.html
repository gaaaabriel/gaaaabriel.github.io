understanding the 100 gecs ep

Part 1: Practical Song Analysis

Introduction:

I love this ep. It’s in my top 5 musical projects of the moment and i truly don’t know why. it fills me with an energy that i can’t explain, a feeling that nothing else makes me feel. i don’t have to understand it to appreciate it but i’m still going to try to find out what this ep does to make me love it so much

This Part of the Thesis will be focused on analysing every song from the ep on a 5 point basis:
-Melody
-Percussions / Other Instrumental Elements
-Sound Design
-Lyrical Content
-Transitional Noise Ending
